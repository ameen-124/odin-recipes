Fried Rice

Chicken Fried Rice is a flavorful and satisfying dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with tender pieces of chicken, fresh vegetables, and aromatic seasonings. This one-pan meal combines the savory taste of soy sauce with the crunch of vegetables like peas, carrots, and bell peppers, all brought together with garlic and green onions. Quick to prepare and easily customizable, Chicken Fried Rice is perfect for a weeknight dinner or as a delicious way to use up leftover rice and chicken. Enjoy it as a standalone meal or as a side dish to complement your favorite Asian-inspired dishes

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, diced or shredded
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (like peas, carrots, and corn)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (or any cooking oil)
  • 2 eggs (optional)
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Make sure all ingredients are prepped and ready to go. If using day-old rice, break up any clumps.
  2. Heat Oil: In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat
  3. Cook Chicken: Add the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until heated through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Scramble Eggs (Optional): If using eggs, crack them into the skillet and scramble until fully cooked. Remove and set aside with the chicken.
  5. Saut√© Vegetables: In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed, then add the mixed vegetables and garlic (if using). Stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Add Rice: Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. Stir well to combine with the vegetables.
  7. Season: Pour the soy sauce over the rice and mix thoroughly. Add the cooked chicken and scrambled eggs back into the skillet. Stir everything together and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add Green Onions: Stir in the chopped green onions and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve: Remove from heat and serve hot. Enjoy your Chicken Fried Rice!
